# Card:
Cards are used to group related information in a layout. They are used to display information in a structured way.\
They are the most common way to display content in a design system.\
It's just a container for Header, Body and Footer.

## Variants:
There aren't that many variants for you to be indecisive.\
Choose the one that fits your design system the best.\
Default is Contained
